Our Company

At 21 Knights we aim to be Africa's leading Engineering, Construction, and Project Management organization. our pledge is to deliver extraordinary results for our customers and our team. This pledge is underpinned by our ability to design for the world, inscribe our commitment with our actions, and construct with boundless imagination.

21knight Company is a global, comprehensive construction and facilities-related solutions firm. Building upon the success and proven track record of more than 15 years of experience, our best-in-class teams deliver complex, geographically dispersed projects across the Africa. and internationally. Strengthened by our global experience and depth of expertise, we bring the benefits of these experiences and adapt them to our local communities. Across the globe, our teams are committed to the highest standards of safety and quality with a commitment to exceed our clients’ expectations.
